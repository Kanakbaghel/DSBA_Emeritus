# Curated Resources on Supply Chain, Customer Analytics, Finance, and AI-Driven Data Analysis

This resources curates a collection of articles, guides, and academic papers on supply chain metrics, demand forecasting, customer segmentation and analytics, financial statement reading, generative AI in data workflows, prompt engineering for LLMs, and synthetic data generation. These resources offer practical insights, theoretical frameworks, and advanced techniques for professionals in business analytics, data science, and AI.

### Generative AI in Data Analysis
| Title | Resource Link | Description |
|-------|---------------|-------------|
| **“It’s like a rubber duck that talks back”: Understanding Generative AI-Assisted Data Analysis Workflows through a Participatory Prompting Study (Ian Drosos, Advait Sarkar, et al., 2024)** | [arXiv Paper](https://arxiv.org/html/2407.02903v1) | Investigates how generative AI tools assist users during data analysis, focusing on two key stages—information foraging (finding and preparing data) and sensemaking (interpreting results and forming hypotheses). Highlights both the advantages (accelerated exploration, idea generation) and challenges (crafting effective prompts, validating AI outputs). |
| **AI, Humans, and Data Science: Optimizing Roles Across Workflows and the Workforce (Richard Timpone & Yongwei Yang, 2025)** | [arXiv Paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.11597) | Proposes a framework for integrating analytic, generative, and agentic AI with human expertise in data science workflows. Examines the shifting balance between automation and human oversight, addressing trade-offs, ethical considerations, and the evolving division of responsibilities in future analytical ecosystems. |
